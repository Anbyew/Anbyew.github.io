Emily Yuwei Bao

Hi! I am a PhD student at the University of Michigan, Department of Computer Science and Engineering. I study Natural Language Processing (NLP). My research focuses on human cognition inspired multimodal grounded language acquisition and pragmatic generation. I am supervised by Prof. Joyce Chai in the Situated Language and Embodied Dialogue (SLED) Lab.
